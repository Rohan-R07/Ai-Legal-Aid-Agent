The Motor Vehicles Act, 1988 (amended in 2019) governs traffic rules and penalties in India.

Key Points for Challans (Fines):
- A traffic challan can be issued on the spot or electronically.
- You have the right to see the evidence if it's an electronic challan (e.g., a photo of the violation).
- You can pay challans online or in court.

Contesting a Challan:
- If you believe a challan was issued incorrectly, you can contest it in a traffic court.
- Website for checking and paying challans: https://echallan.parivahan.gov.in/
- Office: Virtual Court or the designated Traffic Court in your city.
- Process: The process is initiated through the virtual court link found on the e-challan website.
