You can try the following prompts to see how the Legal Aid Assistant responds to different types of issues.

### For Traffic-related issues:
"I was stopped by the traffic police, and they took my car keys. They are asking for a bribe to release them. What should I do?"

### For Employment-related issues:
"My employer is making me work 12 hours a day but is not paying me for overtime. Is this legal?"

### For Consumer-related issues:
"I bought a product from a store, and they charged me more than the MRP. When I asked for a refund, they refused. What are my rights?"

### For Civil-related issues:
"My neighbor is building a wall on my property, and they are not listening to me. What legal action can I take?"

These prompts are designed to trigger the different categories in the prototype mode of the application. You can also try your own prompts to see how the agent responds.
